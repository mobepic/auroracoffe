OUR STORY

From Seed
to Soul

At Aurora, we believe every cup tells a story. Our journey begins at origin — working directly with smallholder farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra to source the world's finest green coffee.

Each bean is roasted in small batches at our Brooklyn roastery, where our master roasters coax out the unique character of every origin. The result? Coffee that transcends the ordinary.
